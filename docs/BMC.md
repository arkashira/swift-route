# Business Model Canvas – **swift‑route**

| **Key Partners** | **Key Activities** | **Key Resources** |
|------------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| • **Financial Institutions** – banks, credit unions, payment processors that need fast, low‑cost cross‑border flows. <br>• **Regulatory Bodies** – KYC/AML, sanctions, and compliance partners. <br>• **Liquidity Providers & FX Platforms** – market makers, liquidity pools, and crypto‑asset exchanges. <br>• **Cloud & Edge Infrastructure** – AWS, GCP, Azure, and edge‑compute providers for low‑latency deployment. <br>• **AI & Data Partners** – providers of real‑time market data, transaction feeds, and risk analytics. | • **Platform Development & Maintenance** – continuous improvement of the AI‑driven routing engine, low‑latency micro‑services, and secure API gateway. <br>• **Data Engineering & Model Training** – ingesting transaction, market, and compliance data; training and fine‑tuning routing models. <br>• **Compliance & Risk Management** – ensuring KYC/AML, sanctions, and regulatory reporting. <br>• **Customer Success & Integration** – onboarding banks, providing SDKs, and offering 24/7 support. <br>• **Business Development & Partnerships** – securing new institutional clients and liquidity partners. | • **AI‑Routing Engine** – vLLM‑based inference pipeline, custom routing models, and reinforcement‑learning loop. <br>• **Low‑Latency Infrastructure** – containerized micro‑services, edge‑compute nodes, and CDN. <br>• **Data Lake & Model Repository** – pgvector‑backed knowledge base, transaction logs, and model artifacts. <br>• **Security & Compliance Stack** – encryption, audit trails, and regulatory tooling. <br>• **Talent & Culture** – experienced AI/ML engineers, fintech compliance experts, and customer‑centric product managers. |

| **Value Proposition** | **Customer Segments** | **Channels** |
|------------------------|-----------------------|--------------|
| • **Ultra‑Fast Cross‑Border Payments** – 10‑fold reduction in latency compared to legacy SWIFT/ISO 20022 paths. <br>• **Cost‑Optimized Routing** – dynamic selection of the cheapest path (FX, liquidity, and network fees) via AI. <br>• **AI‑Driven Risk & Compliance** – real‑time sanctions, AML, and fraud checks embedded in the routing decision. <br>• **Seamless Integration** – REST/GraphQL APIs, SDKs for Java, Python, and Node.js; plug‑and‑play with existing core banking systems. <br>• **Transparent Reporting** – real‑time dashboards, audit logs, and regulatory reports. | • **Large Banks & Credit Unions** – institutions looking to modernize cross‑border flows. <br>• **Payment Service Providers (PSPs)** – fintechs that need to offer low‑latency, low‑cost international payments. <br>• **Digital‑Asset Platforms** – crypto exchanges and wallets requiring instant settlement across borders. <br>• **Remittance Companies** – micro‑payment operators seeking cost advantage and speed. | • **Direct Sales & Enterprise Account Management** – dedicated sales team for large banks and PSPs. <br>• **Partner Ecosystem** – integration partners (core banking vendors, fintech accelerators). <br>• **Industry Events & Webinars** – fintech conferences, SWIFT events, and blockchain summits. <br>• **Online Presence** – website, technical blog, and open‑source community on GitHub. |
