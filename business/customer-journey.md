```markdown
# Customer Journey Map for Swift-Route

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Banks and payment providers experience delays and high costs in cross-border transactions.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about innovative solutions; existing systems are entrenched.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion about options, skepticism about new solutions.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content showcasing the benefits of AI-driven payment routing; case studies from early adopters.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with educational content (e.g., downloads, video views).

---

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Decision-makers begin researching alternatives to their current payment processing systems.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options; difficulty in comparing features and costs; concerns about integration with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful but cautious; desire for clarity and assurance.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Interactive comparison tools; personalized consultations to address specific needs; transparent pricing models.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and engagement with comparison tools.

---

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users sign up for a trial or demo of Swift-Route.
- **Friction Points**: Complexity of initial setup; potential technical issues during integration; lack of immediate support.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety; apprehension about the learning curve.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Onboarding support with dedicated account managers; easy-to-follow setup guides; responsive customer service.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user feedback scores during the trial period.

---

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users decide to fully implement Swift-Route after a successful trial.
- **Friction Points**: Resistance to change from internal teams; concerns about long-term commitment.
- **User Emotions**: Relief and satisfaction; cautious optimism about future performance.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Celebratory onboarding events; success stories shared within the organization; continuous training and support.
- **Metric**: Time to full implementation and user satisfaction scores post-adoption.

---

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users explore additional features or services offered by Swift-Route.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about the value of additional features; potential budget constraints.
- **User Emotions**: Curiosity; desire for more value from the existing solution; cautious about additional spending.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Tailored recommendations based on usage patterns; exclusive offers for existing customers; regular updates on new features and benefits.
- **Metric**: Rate of upsells and cross-sells; customer lifetime value (CLV).

```
